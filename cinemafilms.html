Discovery

What is Cinema Couture About script... "Real. People. Stories Your story is what makes you unique. It’s what defines you against the noise of the world. My true strength is finding that story….your story. Listening to why you do things, watching your face light up and your voice change as you get excited about what you’re doing, telling the world who you are and explaining your purpose. This isn’t marketing. This is focusing your story so it’s engaging, entertaining, visually beautiful, and makes an impact on the audience.
Here's my story I started in wedding films in 2005. Back then all we did was to record what happened. I learned over the years that there was much more to be done. Who is this couple? Why is everyone so happy for them? It made me think about my work differently. It became less about the things or what was happening and became more about the people. I realized a few years ago that businesses, products and organizations are the same. They’re about the people. They’re about making a connection. And frankly, telling that story is what is important. My expertise is not in marketing or branding. My expertise is in getting to the heart of who you are and sharing that heart with the world. I’m very proud of the work we do. The reason I called the company Cinema Couture is we take the position that every film has it’s own personality. Every couple wants their story told a little differently. Every event is about something. Every product or business is unique. We understand how to find that story and custom make a film that reflects your true personality."
